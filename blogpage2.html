Educational Platforms transforms skill set

12 Jan 2022
~ Ritesh Sinha

Recently I was in an online webinar where speakers talking about how to acquire industries skills and how these skills going to help building your carrier.

I want to talk about that what are industries skills?

We all know what the speed at which technologies are changing, and if we are not evolving our selves with respect to changes, we will be left behind the world eventually.
Apart from what is our academic routine irrespective of our specialization (like in my case I’m pursuing mechanical engineering) if we will spend at least 1-2 hours on learning these industries skills it will build a lot of confidence in you to represent yourself.
And nowadays. If you want to learn about anything either about technology or anything, there is a lot of resources out there to help you, it is just a one click away, also there are a lot of courses at very affordable price.
There are many great online learning platforms and courses available on internet including – Udemy, Coursera, Skillshare, Udacity etc. Millions of people are registered online for the courses like Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning (ML), Data Science and many more. Several reports show that professional need to acquire AI and data science skills to be relevant in the future workplace.

The increasing contribution by technology has propelled the four industrial revolutions:
Industry 1.0 – steam power, mechanisation, and the weaving loom
Industry 2.0 – electrical energy, mass production and assembly lines
Industry 3.0 – automation, computers, electronics, and robotics revolutionise organisations
Industry 4.0 – cyber-physical systems, networks, and the internet of thing.

Upgrade your skills to increase employability:
Upgrading yourself with career relevant programs and certificates prepare learners for high-demand jobs in the market. The job market in India is highly competitive and candidates need to keep pace with the changing workplace. Employers are also acknowledging the importance for stackable credentials. Hence, its very important to learn new skills to increase employability and boost business outcomes.
